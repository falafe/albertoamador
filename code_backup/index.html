Hello! I am

an Industrial Designer who loves to learn of every experience of life and enhance as a professional and human.

I am not a creative guy, even a great designer, I am just somebody who loves to maintain the design guidelines and take a process to achieve a work.

We can design every part of our life but...

Think before act

With the passage of time and projects, I adopted several methodologies and adapted them to my designs, making a design process of 80% of think and 20% of act.

I deem necessary to have a design process, even whether the project is a simple task.

Think

< time > time to start the project
h hear the user (brief)
i information about the theme (similar/existing)
n needs identifed of the custumer (insights)
k knowledge acquired (analysis of the ideas and prioritize what can solve the need) break;

act

a apply the broad knowledge acquired to make alternatives (sketch)
c create prototype and iterate (loop test before delivery)
< /time > time to deliver.

*Those steps can vary due to the kind of project.

HEPD Packaging

This was a very exiting and funny project, we proposed different alternatives to a new bracket kit package. With our packaging guidelines we presented different alternatives regarding marketing requirements. The last step where I worked, was the end of the alts measured for the quotation.
